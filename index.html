Indo Wings Channel Partner

Indo Wings Channel Partner form a trusted global extension of Indo Wings by representing us in the regions and languages that they know best.

We dedicate a lot of effort to building and maintaining strong working partnerships. As a result, we trust our Channel Partner to be fully committed to actively selling and promoting Indo Wings. They are in regular contact with us to help develop our regional sales strategies.

Does this sound like an appealing and realistic opportunity for your company?

What Indo Wings offers

  • A higher profit percentage on resold Indo Wings products.
  • A monthly newsletter, detailing company news, product updates and novel use cases.
  • Access to a Partner Portal. This is a management hub for purchasing licenses, tracking sales, marketing material, support and communication.
  • Online endorsement as a Indo Wings Channel Partner.
  • High priority technical support.

Commitment is required

  • A strong sales ability. This is proven when a company achieves a set target set whilst working as a Indo Wings Referral Agent, or by committing to an initial investment in the partnership.
  • A sufficient understanding of Indo Wings software. At least one member of staff pass our online exam: Indo Wingsmapper Essentials.
  • A face-to-face meeting with Indo Wings.

Concept of Partnership

  • Indo Wings works closely with its Channel Partner to develop an successful strategy in each region.
  • Sales targets will be set by Indo Wings; forecasting is required from the Channel Partner.
  • A yearly plan for marketing, advertising and event attendance will be discussed.
  • The Channel Partner must advertise Indo Wings products on their website.

Potential

  • The opportunity to increase profit percentage as sales targets are achieved.
  • The opportunity to influence the growth of photogrammetric software in your region.
